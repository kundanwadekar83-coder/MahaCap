Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is MahaCap an official CAP portal? ▾
No. MahaCap is an independent practice tool to help candidates rehearse filling their option form. Always submit your real preferences on the official CAP portal.
Is it free to use? ▾
Yes, MahaCap is completely free for students to practice their option form.
Will my data be saved if I come back later? ▾
Yes. Once you sign in, your profile and option list are saved to your account and sync whenever you log back in.
Can I download my option list? ▾
Yes — you can export a branded PDF of your full list, a combined detail report, or a CSV file from the Summary page.
